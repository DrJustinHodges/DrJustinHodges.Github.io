ABOUT

My name is Justin Hodges and I am the AI/ML specialist at Siemens Digital Industries. I earned my bachelors, masters, and Ph.D in mechancial engineering at the University of Central Florida in thermofluids. I grew up in a Siemens Energy turbomachinery lab characterizing heat transfer, fluid mechanics, and turbulence in gas turbine secondary flow systems. My masters and doctoral research was on film cooling flow-fields, predicting turbulence and thermal fields with advanced turbulence modeling and machine learning approaches. During an internship in Princeton NJ, I fell in love with AI and have been building my technical proficiency with such since.

I made this web page because I have an insatiable passion for digital innovation. I wanted to document some of my select projects which apply machine learning approaches to simulation problems and datasets.
